About Us

Back in 2022. No-no, back into 2020, I had little knowledge of the services we provide now, despite my background in civil engineering. For the time pass during COVID-19 pandemic, I joined Rama & Sons Construction company, where I worked on-site for a project [ITI Building] in my area. This hands-on experience, multiplied with my growing expertise in BIM and 3D software, opened new opportunities for me.

By 2022, friends and relatives began seeking my help to plan and design their homes for free. This led me to slowly gain more experience, and in 2023, I took the exciting step to expand our offerings. We began providing services in house planning, 3D designing, cost estimates for bank loans, and construction consultancy with the name RS+ ArchetiQ as design studio.

However, this journey hasn't been easy. I realized, while my designs were perfect, contractors often failed to bring them to execution. This is a turn off, left clients unsatisfied with the final results, which was a huge disappointment for me.

I believe in giving my clients the home of their dreams, and this is why I have decided to take full control over the construction process. Moving forward, we will be offering complete construction services with the new name ArchetiQ Construction. This means that all your projects will be managed, built, and supervise directly by our team, ensuring that every aspect is handled with care and precision.
